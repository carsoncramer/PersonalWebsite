Welcome to OreDinners! We're thrilled that you've decided to use our app to find free food around the Colorado School of Mines campus. We want to make sure that you have the best possible experience while using our app, which is why we've put together this customer support page. If you have any questions or issues while using OreDinners, you can find answers to some common questions below. If you don't find the answer you're looking for, please don't hesitate to contact us directly at ctcramer@mines.edu. \n How do I use OreDinners? \n Using OreDinners is easy! Simply download the app from the app store and sign up using your Colorado School of Mines email address. Once you're signed in, you'll see posts with locations telling you where free food is available. When you're ready to grab some free food, simply head to the location and enjoy! \n I'm having trouble logging in. What should I do? \n If you're having trouble logging in to OreDinners, make sure that you're using your Colorado School of Mines email address. If you're still having trouble, try resetting your password using the "forgot password" link on the login screen. If you continue to experience issues, please contact us and we'll be happy to assist you. \n How do I report an issue with the app? \n If you encounter an issue with the OreDinners app, please contact us at ctcramer@mines.edu Please provide as much detail as possible about the issue you're experiencing, including any error messages you may have seen. Our team will review your report and work to resolve the issue as quickly as possible. \n Is my information secure? \n We take the security of your information seriously. All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using industry-standard SSL/TLS technology. We do not share your personal information with any third parties without your explicit consent. \n Thank you for using OreDinners! If you have any further questions or concerns, please don't hesitate to contact us using the form below.
